Eryn
===
by TemplateExpress (@templateexpress, templateexpress.com)

Eryn is a clean and professional new WordPress theme based on the popular eryn Pro premium theme. Powered by Underscores Starter Theme, Eryn offers plenty of customizable options presented in a simple to use manner.



###Customizer Instructions

Just follow the handy instructions located on our documentation page at http://templateexpress.com/eryn/documentation/

###Fully responsive

Eryn is fully responsive, working well on everything from your phone to your large monitor.
